<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Phosphorus-based Flame Retardant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Additive Flame Retardants</li><li>Reactive Flame Retardants</li><li>Synergist Flame Retardants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phosphorus-based flame retardant market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Additive Flame Retardants** are incorporated into materials without chemical bonding, providing flexibility and easy processing.  </p><p>2. **Reactive Flame Retardants** chemically bond with the substrate during polymerization, enhancing thermal stability and effectiveness.  </p><p>3. **Synergist Flame Retardants** enhance the performance of other flame retardants, often enabling lower overall usage and improving the effectiveness of the fire protection system, providing a more sustainable solution.</p></p>
