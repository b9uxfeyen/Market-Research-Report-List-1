<p><ul><li>Flat Airbag Fabric</li><li>OPW Airbag Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive airbag textile market comprises primarily two types: flat airbag fabric and OPW (One-Part Weaving) airbag fabric. Flat airbag fabric is a traditional option, characterized by its simple structure and ease of production, providing essential protection during deployment. In contrast, OPW airbag fabric features a more complex, woven design that enhances durability and performance by offering superior strength and better gas retention. Both types play critical roles in ensuring passenger safety and contribute to the overall effectiveness of airbag systems in vehicles.</p></p>
