<p><p>The coated paper market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical and woodfree. Mechanical coated paper is produced using a combination of wood pulp and mechanical processes, offering better bulk and opacity, making it ideal for everyday printing applications. In contrast, woodfree coated paper is made from chemical pulp, resulting in a smoother surface and higher brightness, which enhances print quality. This type is preferred for high-quality printing, such as magazines and brochures, where visual appeal is crucial.</p></p>
