<p><ul><li>Concentric Type</li><li>Overlap Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cast resin dry-type transformer market consists of two primary types: concentric and overlap types. Concentric type transformers feature coils arranged in concentric layers, providing efficient heat dissipation and improved electrical performance. They are suitable for space-constrained environments. Overlap type transformers, on the other hand, use overlapping winding techniques, enhancing insulation properties and reducing losses. Both designs offer safety advantages, are fire-resistant, and have low maintenance needs, making them ideal for industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
